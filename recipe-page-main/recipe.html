Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meat.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 Minutes
  • Preparation: 5 Minutes
  • Cooking: 5 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Salt, To Taste
  • Pepper, To Taste
  • 1 Tablespoon Of Butter Or Oil
  • Optional Fillings: Cheese, Vegetables, or Meat

Instructions

  1. Crack 2-3 large eggs into a bowl. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to the eggs. You can also add a splash of milk or cream for a fluffier texture.
  2. Prepare the Fillings: If you’re adding vegetables or meat, chop them into small pieces. If you’re using raw vegetables or meat, sauté them in a little butter or oil over medium heat until they’re cooked. Set them aside.
  3. Heat the Pan: A small to medium-sized non-stick skillet works best. Place the pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil. Swirl the pan to coat the bottom evenly.
  4. Cook the Eggs: Once the butter is melted and the pan is hot, pour the beaten eggs into the pan.
  5. Stir and Lift: Use a spatula to gently stir the eggs, pulling them from the edges toward the center. Tilt the pan to allow the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges. Once the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, stop stirring and smooth out the surface.
  6. Add the Fillings: Evenly distribute your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette. If using cheese, sprinkle it over the fillings.
  7. Fold the Omelette: Use your spatula to gently fold the omelette in half over the fillings.
  8. Finish Cooking: Let the omelette cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute to melt the cheese and finish cooking the eggs. The omelette should be slightly golden on the outside but still tender inside.
